Smart Travel

Phase One

Smart Traffic Lights

Cameras will be installed on every traffic light which would monitor the traffic density at each junction and using machine learning would detect different objects such as cars, people, animals and also count the number of each object, then depending upon the density of cars on each juntion, the traffic light would stay green for the lane with higher number of cars, which would help in clearing the traffic more efficiently.

This feature would be useful in office hours. with help of this system we can reduce the travel time of of office workers by a significant amount. this system would also help people going through busy traffic through different narrow streets, and would also help in festival season.

A live feed from these cameras will directly get relayed to police headquarters so that they can monitor the traffic themselves and have a better control over traffic violations.

Image Recognition

Accident Recognition

These cameras will help us detect accidents and also notify the required authorities at the time of emergency. An alert will be sent to the nearest police station and hospital at the time of emergency so that the situation is cleared as fast as possible and also save lives.

These cameras will also help in catching people who dont follow traffic rules such as overspeeding, jumping a red light, taking a wrong side fot travel.

Accident Recognition

App Development

The data regarding the density of traffic at different places at different point of time will be saved and using the machine learning agorithm we will be able to predict the traffic density at different places.

An application will be build based on this data which will help us guide people through the city by choosing the best possible path for them.

This system has a potential to be even more accurate that google maps as google maps is made for the people all around the globe using satellites, but our app will be based on India specific data collected using ground cameras over time.
This would give us a plethora of new avenues to explore such faster more accurate routes, cheaper routes and safer routes.

Route Calculation

Phase Two

Fastest Route

Using the data collected we can guide people on what routes to take at what time of the day. This will take the user from point A to point B more quickly that other methods.

Users can also opt for shortest route. This would taka a user from point A to B through the shortest path possible. This feature will help people save on fuel. So, this feature can be used by people running low on petrol or diesal or people who care for the environment.

Another option would be to choose for the most convenient route which would take the people through areas with least traffic density and wide roads. This feature would be for those people who like to drive and are unable to enjoy it because of high traffic.

Fastest Route

Safest Route

Choosing the safest route will be really useful, especially during night time, many at times people take roads which are less busy due to number of reasons, but using the Safest Route feature, the application will guide people through areas which are busier which make the people safer. This feature can used by cab drivers, which would instill a sense of safety in their passengers.

Safest Route

Cheapest Route

Choosing the cheapest route option would guide people on how to reach their destination in the cheapest way possible. This will be achieved by suggesting the use of public transport such as metro, buses, autos etc. This will promote the use of public transport which in turn is better for the environment.

Cheapest Route

Phase Three

Tourism

The application will also have an option to switch to tourist mode which can be accessed by the native citizens but would be targeted towards foreigners who visit India.

Tourists will be provided with the information regarding the famous tourist spots and how to visit them at what time. The app would also notify the tourists regarding the famous restaurants to visit near them during breakfast, lunch and dinner time.

Tourist Mode

Tourists Routes

The tourists can select their destination and the application will show them the most convinent route possible with least traffic, in order to add to their experience by taking them through beautiful roads and areas so that they can see what a beautiful country India truly is.

The application will also show them different ways that they can take to reach their destination along with the price for each mode of transport so that they dont get scammed.

Tourist Routes

Booking

The application will have a provision to book cabs and also hotels to give the tourists a full experience and make this application as the one stop application for them.

Booking

Self Driving Cars

These camaras could also be made to connect with self driving cars once the car gets in a certain range. After establishing a connection with the car the cameras can give them information regarding their surroundings making them more smarter. The cameras will also fix a huge drawback of self driving cars which is to identify traffic lights. While communication with these cars, the cameras will be able to stop them if the light is red and make them move if the light is green without the car having to look for a traffic light and its colour constantly.
